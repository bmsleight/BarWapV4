    Boys Beds

    A proper engineering solution to limited space

    Connor's Bed Bus

    The original bed. Made because in Connor's room is over the stairs and there is an awkward shape which stop a normal bed be placed on the right hand side. The solution was to build a bunk bed around this shape. This gives space fro the bed, a nice hide-out under the bed and all in the shape of a bus.





    Charlie Classroom Shop Bed

    The other part of the bargain, was that Charlie had a bed, which he helped specify. Charlie wanted; space to play shop, space to play in a classroom and a slide.

    I also wanted space for two beds (proper bunk bed top and bottom) and the lower bed to be able to lift up. d all in the shape of a bus.
