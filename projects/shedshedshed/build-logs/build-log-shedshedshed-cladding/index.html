    Build Log ShedShedShed - Cladding

    The Build log for my Man Cave

    Time to start the cladding

    By cladding I mean anything that goes on the frame. A great reference for timber build found during my google research means I am planning to have:

    • Cladding
    • Battens to crate air circulation
    • Water-proof layer (House-wrap) - blocks water allows condensation to escape
    • Isolation
    • Vapour layer
    • Internal boards

    Good Start

    Used my new nailer to staple the House-Wrap and then add the battens to oen side of the shed.




    Water proof

    Completed the water proof layer - just need to finish the battening.





    Mobile Elevated Working Platform

    Getting and and down from the roof is hard, a Mobile Elevated Working Platform (MEWP) would be ideal. Alas these and scaffolding towers cost a far penny. So with the spare timber left I build a MEWP.
